HISTORY

In the summer of 2022, while learning as student in Belgrade, designer discovered new passion, sharing a love of adventures, exploring, and art. With the help of their friends at BD, they formed brand, a diverse, experience-and-share, make-it-from-scratch, do-it-together all over Belgrade's famous and infamous spots. It later became a creative studio, in a room they rented in center of Belgrade.

In the years since, we worked on a wide array of unique projects with some of the best people from Belgrade and beyond. We strive to keep our work unique and smart, with room for a little magic. You can find us anywhere, and if the voyage is the right fit, we'd love to work with you. Feel free to contact us below.

How to see more about product?

Go to page product by clicking on the image of art you want to read more about. There is a shortcut - when you put you pointer over art product you will see "Quick view" option. Click on it.

How to add to cart?

Go to page product by clicking on the image of art you want to purchase. When page opens, next to images, there is a button "add to cart".

How to view cart?

At the top of site, in navigation menu there is an cart icon. When you click cart icon, cart with your shopping list will open.

How to add buy / checkout / finish shopping?

When you open cart on icon in the navigation on the top of the site, you will see check-out button bellow, but only if your shopping list is not empty!
