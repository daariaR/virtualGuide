How to get here?


By Plane

+381 192 192 921092

The nearest airport to Belgrade is Belgrade (BEG). Nikola Tesla Airport A1 Shuttle operates a bus from Belgrade Nikola Tesla Airport to Belgrade Main station every 30 minutes. Tickets cost RSD 300 - RSD 360 and the journey takes 30 min. Bus Plus also services this route every 30 minutes. Alternatively, you can take a train from Timisoara (TSR) to Belgrade via Cartier Aeroport, Timişoara Nord, Stamora Moraviţa, and Vrsac in around 5h 29m.




By Bus

+381 192 192 921092

Using the public transport within the city will get you much faster to your destination than using a car. Public transport is pretty efficient; there are cca 176 bus lines (27 night lines), 12 tram lines and 8 trolleybus lines. How frequently these lines run depends on the route and the period of the day; the waiting time is between 3 and 30 minutes. Each station, at least those in the centre, has a bus schedule.
